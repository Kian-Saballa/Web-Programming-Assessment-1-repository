Lasagna Recipe

This well-loved recipe features a rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta filling, and layers of pasta baked to golden perfection.

Ingredients

  • Meat sauce
    • 1.25 lbs Italian sausage
    • 0.75 lbs ground beef
    • 1 diced onion
    • 3 minced garlic cloves
    • 2 cans crushed tomatoes
    • 2 cans tomato paste
    • 2–3 tbsp sugar
    • 3 tbsp parsley
    • 2 tsp dried basil
    • 0.75 tsp fennel seed
    • 0.75 tsp salt
    • 0.25 tsp pepper
  • Cheese filling
    • 1 carton (15 oz) ricotta
    • 1 large egg
    • 1/4 cup parsley
    • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Cheeses for layering
    • 4 cups shredded mozzarella
    • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • Pasta
    • 9 lasagna noodles (cooked al dente)

Instructions

  1. Cook Noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in salted water until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. Make Meat Sauce: Brown the meat and onions in a large pot, add garlic, then stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, and spices; simmer uncovered for 30 minutes.
  3. Prepare Cheese Mixture: Whisk together the ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, and salt in a bowl.
  4. Layer: In a 13x9-inch baking dish, spread 2 cups of meat sauce, then layer 3 noodles, one-third of the ricotta mixture, 1 cup of mozzarella, and 2 tablespoons of Parmesan; repeat twice, finishing with sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for another 25 minutes until bubbling; let rest for 15 minutes before serving
