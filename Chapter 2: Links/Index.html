Pokémon Legends: Z-A

Plot Overview

Pokémon Legends: Z-A is set in Lumiose City, five years after the events of Pokémon X and Y, during an urban redevelopment project led by Quasartico Inc. to create coexistence zones called Wild Zones for humans and Pokémon. The player arrives as a tourist and quickly becomes involved with Team MZ, a group based at Hotel Z, run by the enigmatic AZ, a 3,000-year-old man accompanied by his unique Eternal Flower Floette.

Key Information

Platform: Nintendo Switch

Release: 2025

Developer: Game Freak
